Lasagna

Description

This classic Italian dish is made with layers of pasta, rich tomato sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 jar of marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Cook ground beef in a pan until browned, then add marinara sauce.
  3. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat layers until ingredients are used up, then top with extra mozzarella.
  6. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 35-40 minutes.
  7. Let cool for 10 minutes, then serve.
