Pizza

Description

Homemade pizza with a crispy crust, tangy tomato sauce, and a generous topping of cheese and pepperoni.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup pepperoni slices
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese and oregano on top.
  5. Add pepperoni slices.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden brown.
  7. Drizzle olive oil on top and serve hot.
